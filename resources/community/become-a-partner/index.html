Partner Ecosystem

The General Bots partner ecosystem connects technology companies, system integrators, and hosting providers who build, deploy, and support AI bot solutions on the General Bots platform. Partners benefit from a growing market of organizations seeking open-source AI automation, a platform that eliminates vendor lock-in for their clients, and a technology stack that reduces delivery time from months to weeks. The ecosystem operates on a principle of shared value: partners deliver client success, and the platform grows through reputation, not restrictive licensing.

  • Access a growing market of organizations seeking open-source AI
  • Deliver client solutions without vendor lock-in constraints
  • Reduce delivery time from months to weeks with the General Bots stack
  • Shared-value model: partners succeed, the platform grows by reputation

Certified Hosting Partners

Certified hosting partners operate General Bots instances for clients who prefer managed deployment over self-hosting. Certification ensures that partners meet infrastructure requirements, maintain platform updates, and provide SLA-backed support. Hosting partners set their own pricing, define their own service tiers, and own the client relationship. General Bots provides the platform, documentation, and technical support channel. The hosting partner provides the operations, monitoring, and first-line support. This model has proven effective for organizations in regulated industries that require AI capabilities but lack internal DevOps capacity.

  • Certification ensures infrastructure, updates, and SLA compliance
  • Partners set their own pricing and service tiers
  • General Bots provides platform, docs, and technical support channel
  • Proven model for regulated industries without DevOps capacity

Solution and Integration Partners

Solution partners build vertical AI bot applications on General Bots — industry-specific packages that combine BASIC scripts, LLM configurations, and channel integrations into turnkey solutions. Integration partners connect General Bots to enterprise systems: ERPs, CRMs, ticketing platforms, and document management systems. Both partner types benefit from the platform open architecture: there are no proprietary APIs to license, no platform fees per deployment, and no restrictions on how solutions are packaged or priced. The only requirement is that partner solutions remain compatible with the General Bots runtime so clients retain portability.

  • Solution partners build vertical AI bot packages on the platform
  • Integration partners connect bots to ERPs, CRMs, and enterprise systems
  • No proprietary API licenses, no per-deployment platform fees
  • Client portability preserved through runtime compatibility

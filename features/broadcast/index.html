Campaigns

Targeted Messaging Campaigns

Create, target, and optimize broadcast campaigns that reach the right audience at the right time.

  • Targeted messaging

    Segment your audience and deliver personalized content to each group for maximum relevance and engagement.

  • Audience segmentation

    Define audience segments based on demographics, behavior, channel preference, or custom attributes.

  • A/B testing

    Test different message variants to identify the most effective content, timing, and call-to-action strategies.

  • Cross-Channel Delivery

    Broadcast simultaneously to WhatsApp, Teams, Web Chat, and Email using a single campaign interface, with automatic content formatting per channel.

Scheduling

Smart Message Scheduling

Plan your broadcasts with flexible scheduling options that respect time zones and audience availability.

  • Timed delivery

    Set precise delivery times for your broadcasts to ensure messages arrive when your audience is most receptive.

  • Recurring campaigns

    Automate repeat campaigns with daily, weekly, or monthly schedules for newsletters, reminders, and updates.

  • Timezone support

    Respect recipient time zones to deliver messages at optimal local times, improving open and engagement rates.

  • Throttling and Rate Limits

    Automatically pace message delivery to comply with WhatsApp/API rate limits and prevent overwhelming human support agents with simultaneous replies.

Analytics

Real-Time Broadcast Analytics

Track delivery, engagement, and conversion metrics to measure and optimize your communication strategy.

  • Delivery tracking

    Monitor message delivery status in real time — sent, delivered, read, and failed — across all channels.

  • Engagement metrics

    Measure clicks, replies, and conversions to understand how your audience interacts with each broadcast.

  • Performance reports

    Generate comprehensive reports comparing campaigns, channels, and segments to refine your strategy.

  • Bot Handoff Tracking

    See exactly how many broadcast recipients engaged with the bot and subsequently required human intervention.

Your company's most valuable asset is its data. But most people can't access it because they don't know SQL. Talk to Data removes this barrier entirely — anyone in your organization can ask questions in plain English and get answers from your databases, spreadsheets, and business systems.

Semantic Schema Mapping

Automatically maps technical database schemas to business concepts for intuitive querying. Users ask about 'sales by region' not 'SELECT SUM(amount) FROM orders GROUP BY region'.

Dynamic Query Generation

Generates complex SQL on the fly, handling multi-table joins, filters, aggregations, and window functions with sub-second execution.

AI-Powered Analysis

The bot doesn't just return numbers — it synthesizes trends, highlights anomalies, and provides context for the data. Charts and visualizations generated automatically.

How It Works

The system works by mapping your database schema to business concepts automatically. A table called 'cust_orders' becomes 'customer orders.' A column called 'ord_total' becomes 'order total.' Users don't need to understand foreign keys, join conditions, or aggregate functions. They just ask: 'How many orders did we get last month?' or 'Show me sales by region for Q3.' The AI generates the SQL, executes it against your database, and returns the results as plain language with optional charts. If the result looks wrong, the user can ask follow-up questions to refine. It's like having a data analyst on call 24/7.

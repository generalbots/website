Content creation is the bottleneck for most marketing teams. You know what needs to be said, but writing it all — in the right tone, for the right audience, at the right length — takes time that most teams don't have. General Bots Content Generation speeds this up without sacrificing quality or brand consistency.

Atomic Templates

Define structure and constraints once in a template, then generate infinite variations that respect your brand guidelines. Templates enforce tone, vocabulary, and compliance rules across all output.

Brand Voice Guardrails

Strict enforcement of tone, vocabulary, and compliance rules. Every piece of generated content is checked against your brand standards before delivery.

SEO-First Logic

Built-in semantic analysis ensures content matches user intent and search engine requirements. Keyword optimization, readability scoring, and meta description generation.

How It Works

The secret is structured templates combined with AI. You define the guardrails — tone of voice, vocabulary, structural requirements, compliance rules — and the AI generates within those boundaries. A human reviews and approves before anything goes live. For a blog post, you specify the topic, target keywords, and length. For a product description, you provide the features and the AI writes the benefits. The system learns from your edits and gets better over time.

Bot Factory

Industrial Scale Bot Deployment.

Mass-produce bots from central templates, manage multi-tenant deployments, and scale your digital workforce from a unified dashboard.

Built for Complexity

The Bot Factory uses a unique "Parent-Child" architecture. Define a single PROMPT.md template and spawn thousands of isolated instances instantly.

  • Atomic Template System

    Update the master template and all child bots receive the update instantly. No manual redeployment required.

  • True Multi-Tenancy

    Complete data isolation. Tenant A's customer data is cryptographically separated from Tenant B on the same core.

  • Scale Without Limits

    Orchestrate hundreds of specialized bots (HR, IT, Sales) under a single management plane.

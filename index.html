Architecture

Three Pillars of Orchestration

AI orchestration + Vector retrieval + Open core — working in harmony.

Pillar 01

AI Orchestration

Connect to any model provider — OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, Groq — or host your own. Dynamic model routing routes simple queries to cheaper models and complex tasks to high-tier models. Intelligent failover ensures zero downtime.

Pillar 02

Vector Retrieval

High-performance semantic memory. High-fidelity embeddings, context-aware chunking that respects document structure, and cross-encoder re-ranking for precision. Sub-second latency across millions of documents.

Pillar 03

Open Source Core

AGPL-3.0 licensed. No vendor lock-in. Full transparency. Self-host on your own infrastructure. Community-driven with 20+ contributors worldwide. Built since 2017.

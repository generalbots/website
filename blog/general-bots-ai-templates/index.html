Ready-to-Use AI Templates

Author: Pragmatismo Team

Starting a bot project from a blank page is the hardest part. You need to define conversation flows, configure LLM prompts, set up channel integrations, handle error states, and implement guardrails — all before you can test a single user interaction. For organizations exploring AI for the first time, this initialization cost is the primary barrier to adoption. General Bots AI Templates eliminate it by providing production-ready starting points for the most common business use cases: customer support automation, e-mail processing, report generation, and internal knowledge retrieval.

Each template is a complete, deployable bot package. It includes BASIC logic for conversation handling, pre-configured LLM prompts optimized for the target use case, channel adapters for the most relevant platforms, and documentation that explains how to customize the behavior for your specific business. The customer support template, for example, includes intent classification, FAQ retrieval, escalation logic, and handoff to human agents — all wired together and ready to deploy on WhatsApp, Teams, or Web Chat within minutes.

Customization is where templates transition from demos to production systems. Every template is designed to be modified in place: you adjust the BASIC logic to match your business rules, you add your own documents to the RAG pipeline, you configure the LLM prompts to reflect your brand voice, and you set the escalation thresholds that match your service level agreements. Because the templates are written in BASIC, not in a proprietary visual builder, you have full visibility into the logic and full control over every behavior. There are no hidden flows, no locked configurations, no vendor-imposed limitations on what you can change.

Deployment examples from the General Bots community illustrate the range of applications. A retail company deployed the customer support template on WhatsApp and reduced first-response time from 12 minutes to under 30 seconds. A logistics firm customized the e-mail processing template to extract shipment details from carrier notifications and update their tracking system automatically. A financial services provider adapted the report generation template to produce daily compliance summaries from transaction data. In each case, the template provided the foundation, and BASIC customization delivered the specific business value. Templates do not replace development — they accelerate it by starting from a working system instead of a blank page.

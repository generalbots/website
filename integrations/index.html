Chat — Your AI Co-Pilot

Real-time WebSocket messaging with LLM integration. WebSocket streaming, multi-channel switching, suggestion chips, and mention support. Agents respond with access to your calendar, files, and contacts.

    Mention files, people, or calendar events directly in chat. The AI pulls the context automatically.

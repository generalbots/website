Beyond Keyword Search

Traditional search looks for words. Vector search looks for *meaning*. General Bots uses embeddings to transform your documents into multi-dimensional vectors, allowing bots to find answers even when the user uses different terminology.

  • High-Fidelity Embedding

    Support for OpenAI, Voyage AI, and local transformers. Your data is mapped into a high-dimensional space where semantic relationships are preserved.

  • Context-Aware Chunking

    Recursive chunking that respects paragraph boundaries and document structure, ensuring that the AI always receives coherent information.

  • Re-ranking & Refinement

    Cross-encoder re-ranking filters out noise and ensures only the most relevant context is used for the final answer generation.
